On behalf of the Ministry of Public Health, Royal Thai Government, I am honored to invite you to participate in the Third International Conference on Dengue and Dengue Haemorrhagic Fever 2013 (Dengue 2013). The conference will be held on 23-25 October 2013 at Imperial Queen’s Park Hotel in Bangkok, Thailand. The theme of this conference is “Global Dengue: Challenge and Promises”.

The past 2 conferences were very successful at Chiang Mai (2001) and Phuket (2008), respectively. This conference aims to bring together academic scientists, academic doctors, leading researchers, practitioners, scholars and students, who interested in the global of Dengue

and Dengue Haemorrhagic Fever, to exchange and share their experiences and research results on aspects of Dengue, and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. The result of Conference will enhance the collaboration of delegates to improve Dengue situation which is one of burden disease for public health.

The Conference comprises 4 parts that are the lectures by noted authorities, plenary session, symposium including invited speakers, oral and poster presentation, and scientific exhibitions. We expect worldwide participants and conference members are encouraged to submit their papers focusing on one of these topics as follows; pathophysiology, diagnosis, clinical management, epidemiological surveillance, laboratory aspects, vector, biology, ecology and control, advocacy for social and community commitment, viro-molecular considerations, and vaccine development.

Please mark these dates on your busy schedule and start making plans to join us for what we hope will has a milestone of the International Conference on Dengue and Dengue Haemorrhagic Fever 2013 together.


 


Professor Emeritus Prasert Thongcharoen
Chair, Scientific Organizing Committee
